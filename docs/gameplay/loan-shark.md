# Loan Shark

Loan Shark is a tribe activity, where each tribe member can risk bonus points by contributing them to the pot. For each 5 bonus points contributed by a tribe, they earn the right to receive +1 points. 4 bonus points receive +0 points.

### Prompt

For this week's immunity ponies, Tribes will not automatically earn +1 points on an immunity win. Instead, each tribe must contribute 4 bonus points to the pot for a +1 points opportunity for the tribe. However, you can contribute more - another 4 bonus points earns you an opportunity to take home +2. 

Here's the twist. **Contributions to the pot are blind** - all contributions are revealed simultaenously. All 4 bonus points are required to earn +1 points. 

### Loan Shark Advantage Scroll

The Loan Shark Advantage gives a tribe an advantage when playing Loan Shark. Instead of contributing 4 points for a +1, they must only contribute 3 points for a +1.
