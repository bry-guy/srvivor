# Individual Pony Auction

The individual pony auction is the merge event in Castaway. Players are able to bid on ponies in a blind auction using their bonus points. Winning players pay the cost of the second highest bidder. Individual ponies work just like tribe ponies - if that player wins immunity, then the player who owns that pony earns bonus points. Individual ponies earn +3 bonus points. Players can collect multiple ponies.

The Loan Shark participates in the Individual Pony Auction, and players may loan extra bonus points from him.

### Prompt

Alright players, it's time. Drop your buffs. Welcome to the merge!

Missing your ponies already? Well fear not - because this week we have the **Individual Pony Auction**! Over the next week, you will have the opportunity to **bid your bonus points** to buy the rights to an individual pony (a player in survivor). Individual ponies **earn +3 bonus points for each immunity win!** 

> To submit your bid, you'll have to use `/castaway bid <player> <points>` when the auction is open. To see what bids are open, you can ask `/castaway activity Individual Pony Auction`.
