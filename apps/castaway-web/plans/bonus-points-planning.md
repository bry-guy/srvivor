# Bonus Points Expansion Plan

Status: `planning`

## Goal

Extend `castaway-web` beyond core draft placement scoring to support bonus scoring rules.

## Scope

- model bonus rules and assignments
- track bonus-related season events
- support auditable score adjustments
- integrate bonus scoring into leaderboard calculation
